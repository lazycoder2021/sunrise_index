AboutDr. Nikita Trehan

Managing Director – Gynaecologist and Laparoscopic Surgeon

Dr. Nikita Trehan Is a renowned Gynecologist and Laparoscopic Surgeon. For the last 20 years she is dedicated to Gynae Endoscopy and Minimally Invasive Gynaecology at “Sunrise Hospitals, INDIA, & Sunrise IMH, Dubai”. She is the Managing Director of Sunrise Hospital, Delhi, Mumbai & International Modern Hospital, Dubai. Dr. Nikita Trehan holds the world record for the largest fibroid remove laparoscopically and for the operating oldest patient, 107 years old. Sunrise Hospital Delhi is the Best Gynecologist Hospital In Delhi.
